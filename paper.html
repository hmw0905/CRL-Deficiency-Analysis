Primary Drivers of FDA Complete Response Letters

This paper presents the full research project, including background, data preparation, SAS workflow, statistical methods, results, limitations, and future directions.

Open Paper PDF

What the paper covers

The paper explains how publicly available FDA Complete Response Letters were manually reviewed, coded into structured deficiency domains and subtypes, and analyzed using SAS.

It also summarizes major findings, including the dominance of CMC deficiencies, differences between NDA and BLA applications, and the presence of multi-domain CRLs.
