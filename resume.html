Featured Research Project

FDA Complete Response Letter Deficiency Analysis

Independent research project analyzing publicly available FDA Complete Response Letters to identify patterns in regulatory deficiencies and application outcomes.

  • Structured and coded 400+ FDA Complete Response Letters into analysis-ready datasets.
  • Developed a classification framework for deficiency domains and subtypes, including CMC, Clinical, Nonclinical, Regulatory, and Statistics.
  • Used SAS for data cleaning, merging, frequency analysis, chi-square testing, co-occurrence summaries, and logistic regression.
  • Built an R Shiny dashboard to make project results interactive and easier to explore.
  • Prepared a conference-style research paper and supplementary statistical results reports.
